# Laboratory Activity: My Student Life Portfolio (Spring Boot)

## Objective
Create a personal portfolio website that presents your student life, experiences, interests, and achievements using Spring Boot and Thymeleaf.

## Minimum Pages Required
- **Home Page (/)**: Introduction, full name, course/year, welcome message, profile photo.
- **About Me (/about)**: Biography, why BSIT, interests/hobbies, goals, at least 2 pictures.
- **My College Journey (/journey)**: Experiences, favorite subjects, challenges, at least 3 pictures.
- **Skills and Interests (/skills)**: Programming skills (Java, HTML, CSS, JS), other interests, icons/images.
- **Photo Gallery (/gallery)**: At least 6 pictures with captions.

## Design Requirements
- Navigation bar
- Consistent design
- Organized layout
- Images properly displayed
- Bonus: Responsive design, animations, hover effects.

## Image Requirements
- At least 10 images total.
